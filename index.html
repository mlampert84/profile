About

I'm a computer programmer based in Berlin with a diverse background that includes work in finance, research and instruction in German language and literature, and software and web development.

The common theme in all my endeavors is my love of systems, specifically a system's ability to provide strutures with which to build new things and invite new perspectives. Whether that has meant exploring price movements in bond markets, teaching the rules and patterns of the German language, or programming web applications, I have always endeavored to leverage the order and predictability of established systems to foster new ways of looking at the world and interacting with it.

I enjoy learning new programming languages and exploring the potential of new libraries and web frameworks. I bring to every one of my projects a passion for inquiry and excellence. Look around my site and if you want to hear more, contact me at marcus DOT lampert AT gmail DOT com.
